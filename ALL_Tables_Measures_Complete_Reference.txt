================================================================================
MEASURE NAMING CONVENTIONS USED
================================================================================

PERCENTAGES & RATES:
   - End with "Percentage" (e.g., FT Student Percentage)
   - Or end with "Rate" (e.g., Participation Rate)
   - Format: 0.0%

COUNTS & TOTALS:
   - Clear descriptive names
   - No special suffix
   - Format: #,0

FINANCIAL:
   - Dollar amounts clearly stated
   - "Revenue", "Budget", "Cost" in name
   - Format: "$"#,0

AVERAGES & MEDIANS:
   - Start with "Average" or "Median"
   - Clear what's being averaged
   - Format: 0.0 or 0.00

INDICES & SCORES:
   - End with "Index" or "Score"
   - Higher = better (unless noted)
   - Format: 0.0% or 0.0
